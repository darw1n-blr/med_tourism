FAQ

What is a medical tourism coordinator?

‚ñº

A medical tourism coordinator is a company that coordinates travel abroad for the purpose of treatment. This is necessary to improve interaction between clinics, patients, insurance and transport organizations.

Why do foreign citizens come to Belarus for treatment?

‚ñº

Medical tourism in Belarus is becoming more and more in demand and popular every year. The country has been accepting foreigners for quite a long time who have decided to undergo a complex operation or undergo an effective course of treatment from our specialists. In recent years, the Republic of Belarus has proven itself as one of the most promising medical tourism destinations in the CIS.
 There are a number of reasons why Belarus has proved one of the most promising medical tourism destinations in the CIS in recent years.
 1) Highly-qualified medical professionals who get advanced training in the world‚Äôs best hospitals.
 2) Exceptional quality of medical services and advanced equipment in hospitals.
 3) Cutting-edge medical technologies and progressive treatment methods.
 4) Reasonable prices for medical services.
 5) Convenient geographical location of Belarus in the center of Europe and well-developed transport communication.
 6) Mild climate that does not require acclimatization and that contributes to faster rehabilitation.
 7) Visa-free regime.
